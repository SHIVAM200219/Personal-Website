    • Descriptions - Why Data Science ? And How IITG & MFSDSAI meets the requirements?
    • A spline plot to show Citations Per Year Counts for our Core Faculty.

  • Professors :- Contains Informations of all Professors
    • One can visit Faculty Portfolio Website by clicking on respective name.
    • One can send email, make Phone Call by clicking respective links.
    • One can know the research areas of respective Professor.
  • Research :- Contains Research Paper related informations
    • One can search for any of the following fields : (i) Professor Name (ii) Research Domain (iii) Year Published (iv) Research Paper You can apply multiple fields at a time to get intersection of all the individual results.
    • One can select type of plotting for data visualization of No. of Research Papers per Professor & No. of research Papers per Domain.
    • A checkbox named Only MFSDSAI, can be ticked to get only those papers which were published under MFSDSAI.
    • You will get a Tabular results as well as a plotting. Tabular results contain following fields : (i) Professor Name (ii) Title of Research Paper (iii) Citation Counts (iv) Authors (v) Publication Date (vi) Publisher (vii) Conference/Journal (viii) Domain
    • In Tabular results, Professor Name contain a link to Professor Portfolio Website & Title of Research Paper contain a link to respective Research Paper./li>
  • Query :- Contains Research Informations related to Faculty, Year and Domain
    • One can search for a faculty, will get following results:
      • A bar plot of Citation Count per Year.
      • A doughnut Plot of all Co-author worked with that faculty.
      • Most Citated Paper with Citation Counts, Most active domain, Publishers, Domain of first and last papers of that faculty.
    • One can search for a year, will get following results:
      • A bar plot of Paper Count in each domain.
      • Most explored domain in that year and Publishers of that year.
    • One can search for a domain, will get following results:
      • A bar plot of Paper Count in each year.
      • Most explored domain in that domain and Publishers of that domain.
  • Analysis :- Contains few analysis of Mehta Family School of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence
    • A bar plot of paper counts in different Research Areas.
    • A bar plot of paper counts of each Professors.
    • A bar plot of Citation Counts of each professors (lifetime and since 2018).
    • A pie plot of Distribution of Domains of Research Papers.
    • A bar plot of paper counts of frequent Publishers.
